I want to make a Zine

About feelings of death

and suicide. A zine about all the beautiful things keeping us stuck living.

I choose to live in a world where the suicidal can be open and be proud. One where if we choose to die people won't assume we were broken, lost, and needing to be found.

To be a part of this you have to promise yourself: No surprises. You'll talk about it. You'll let people know.

If we're gonna try to die then we should try to die happy.

If we're gonna try to die then dying should be a challenge, a great hurdle worth celebrating.
